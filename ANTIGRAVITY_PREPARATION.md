# Antigravity Preparation & MCP-First Future

## The Emergence of Antigravity
The transition from Gemini CLI to Antigravity represents a significant architectural shift toward standardized, interoperable AI engineering. Antigravity brings a more robust runtime environment that aligns with the industry-standard Model Context Protocol (MCP).

## Why EGC is Adapting
EGC was designed with flexibility in mind. By adapting to Antigravity and MCP, we are ensuring that your skills, hooks, and agents are not locked into a single ecosystem but are instead part of a broader, more powerful network of AI tools.

## MCP-First Direction
The future of EGC is MCP-first. This means:
- **Standardized Tools:** Skills will be exposed as MCP tools.
- **Rich Resources:** Knowledge bases and context will be managed as MCP resources.
- **Interoperability:** EGC components will be consumable by any MCP-compliant host (Cursor, Claude Desktop, and others).

---
*EGC is entering a structured transition phase. Continuity and backward compatibility remain our priority.*
