# [ANNOUNCEMENT] EGC Runtime Transition Preparation

## The Ecosystem is Evolving
Hello EGC Community,

We are writing to communicate a significant but positive evolution in the Everything Gemini Code ecosystem. As many of you know, Google is evolving its AI tooling, which includes the transition from Gemini CLI to the new Antigravity runtime.

## Our Commitment to Continuity
EGC is NOT being abandoned. On the contrary, we are entering a structured transition phase to ensure that the framework you rely on remains stable and powerful in this new environment.

## Why We Are Adapting
- **Ecosystem Alignment:** Moving toward Antigravity ensures EGC stays at the cutting edge of AI engineering.
- **MCP Standardization:** Adopting the Model Context Protocol (MCP) will make EGC skills and agents more interoperable and easier to use across different platforms.
- **Stability First:** We are planning this migration carefully. Technical work has NOT started yet; we are currently in the "Legacy Stabilization" phase.

## What This Means for You
- **No Immediate Action Required:** Your current Gemini CLI setups will continue to work.
- **Preservation Guaranteed:** We have tagged the current state as a "Legacy Stable" generation.
- **Technical Transparency:** We have added a Transition Plan and Roadmap to the repository for your review.

We are excited about this next chapter and remain committed to building the best local-first engineering framework for AI.

Stay tuned for more updates as we move into the research and auditing phases.
